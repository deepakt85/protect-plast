Who We Are

Protect Plast Co. LLC is one of the pioneers in the manufacture of high quality Polyethylene Sheets and is one of the largest Suppliers of our product in UAE . At the proud moment of completing 13 years of success and steady growth, PROTECT PLAST renews its commitment of providing quality products with a touch of its professionalism.

Our consistent commitment to develop and deliver premium quality products has made PROTECT PLAST synonymous with quality, reliability & with excellence in customer service.

We have 13+ years of experiences for give
you better results.

Our product quality and pricing strategies are designed to meet local as well as the export market. PROTECT PLAST is dedicated in providing customer satisfaction by manufacturing High Quality Polyethylene Sheets with value added services in a cost effective manner and to deliver our products on time every time.

Quality of our products starts with using the best raw materials and ends with rigorous testing of our finished products. Our products are manufactured as per BS and ASTM standards. The company has a very stringent quality control and each batch of product is thoroughly checked and inspected before dispatch. Various tests are conducted with standard processing equipments in the laboratory using different samples to improve our quality consistently.
